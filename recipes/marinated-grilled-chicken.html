Marinated Grilled Chicken

Quick protein on the grill

Easy recipe that you can grill up in less than 10 min

Ingredients

  • 4 Chicken Thighs
  • Marinade of your choice (italian dressing works great)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Salt

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken and keep in fridge for at least one hour before grilling (overnight preferred)
  2. Transfer marinated chicken to a plate and season with salt, pepper, and garlic salt
  3. Heat grill on medium-high heat (~400 degrees)
  4. Place chicken directly on grill and cook for around 5 minutes or so
  5. Flip chicken and cook an additional 3 minutes or so
  6. Pull chicken from grill once internal temperature reaches 170 degrees
