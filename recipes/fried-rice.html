Fried Rice

Great meal to ease hangovers!

Suprisingly easy and quick to make!

Ingredients

  • Bag of Trader Joes frozen white rice
  • Soy Sauce
  • Butter
  • 1 Egg
  • Small Onion
  • Bag of peas and carrot chunks

Instructions

  1. Heat pan to medium heat
  2. Pour in cooked bag of white rice and add butter
  3. Add in peas and carrots and mix with rice and butter
  4. Turn pan to high and add soy sauce
  5. Fry until rice turns brown
