French Toast

A great snowday recipe!

Easy to make and loaded with flavor, this recipe is a must for an early cold snowed in day!

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread (whole white works best)
  • 2 eggs
  • Butter
  • Vanilla extract
  • Sugar
  • Cinnamon
  • Nutmeg
  • Oat milk

Instructions

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl
  2. Combine sugar, vanilla extract, cinnamon, nutmeg, and oat milk in with eggs and whisk
  3. Heat butter in pan over medium heat until melted
  4. Dip pieces of bread in egg mixture and place in pan
  5. Heat in pan until golden brown on each side
  6. Serve with warm syrup
